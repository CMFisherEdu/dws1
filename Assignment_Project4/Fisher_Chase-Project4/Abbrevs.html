The Battle for Wesnoth: Gamer's Group

This page is dedicated to the tactics, strategy, style and lore of the hit, hex-style, turn-based strategy game: Battle for Wesnoth.

The following are some key terms and abbreviations

ToD
Time of Day, probably the single most important term in the game.
UMC
User-Made Content. Refers to custom campaigns, eras, factions and events.
TC
ZOC
Zone of Control. A game rule - where units cannot move by enemy units in adjacent hexes.
