The Battle for Wesnoth: Gamer's Group

This page is dedicated to the tactics, strategy, style and lore of the hit, hex-style, turn-based strategy game: Battle for Wesnoth.

Many people ask - why play an indie game?

  1. This game is free! - Everybody loves free
  2. There is a dedicated, official multi-player server
  3. Easy to pick-up and get playing
  4. Massively customizable: tons of user-created content
